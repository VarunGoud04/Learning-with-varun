Scient E-Library

Permanently Closed

The Scient E-Library platform has been permanently shut down and is no longer operational. All services, access, and updates have been fully discontinued.

Official Closure Notice

This page serves as the official and final closure notice for the Scient E-Library platform. The project has reached the end of its lifecycle and is no longer maintained, supported, or accessible in any form.

There is no successor platform, continuation, or migration plan associated with Scient E-Library. This website exists solely to communicate the platform’s discontinued status.

What This Means for Previous Users

  • All user accounts and stored data are no longer available.
  • Academic resources, notes, and question banks have been removed.
  • Login, registration, and dashboard features are permanently disabled.
  • No data recovery, reactivation, or support requests can be processed.

About Scient E-Library

Scient E-Library was a student-developed academic resources platform created to support students of Scient Institute by providing organized study materials, previous examination papers, and curriculum-aligned reference content during its active period.

The platform has since been formally discontinued and is no longer part of any active educational or technical initiative.
